Owning a Dog

Why do you want a dog?

It's a simple question, but one many people fail to ask themselves before they get a dog.

Getting a dog just because it's "the thing to do," or because your children have been begging for a puppy can end up being a big mistake.

Remember that you will be responsible for caring for your new family member for up to 15 years.

Is there enough time in your life for a dog?

Dogs are pack animals and you are part of their new pack.

They are companion animals and cannot be ignored just because you're busy or too tired to deal with them.

They require a lot of care, which includes feeding, exercising, and socializing â€“ dogs need companionship every day.

Many wonderful dogs end up in shelters because their owners didn't have the time to train and care for them.

Can you afford a dog?

The costs of caring for a dog throughout her life can be expensive.

Healthcare, training classes, grooming, food, toys, treats, boarding, and other expenses add up fast.
